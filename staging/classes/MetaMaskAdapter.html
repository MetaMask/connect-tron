switchChain

  • switchChain(chainId): Promise<void>

  • Switches the chain for the MetaMask wallet. During the initial connection process by TronWallet, this method can be called multiple times in parallel. And if we call the createSession method multiple times in parallel, it fails. That's why we added a _switchingChain flag to avoid multiple simultaneous calls.
